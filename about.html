Purpose

This offline learning website entitled The Sun intends to provide information and to discuss about the origin of tsunami and its fundamental concepts. It includes the information on how it occurs, its common causes and areas especially in the Philippines which are prone to this. It also covers the different types of tsunamis and the impacts or effects of these to the affected area. Tsunami safety information and tips are also covered to spread awareness to the learners.

Outcomes

Explain how a typhoon develops

Determine how landmasses and bodies of water affect typhoons

Determine ways on how to prepare for incoming typhoons
